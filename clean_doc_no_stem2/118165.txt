produksi varietas hibrida dianggap revolusi hijaurevolusi pangan abad ke20 importance of heterosis the exploitation of heterosis through the development of hybrid cultivars is one of the landmark achievements in plant breeding ever since the work of shull and east in 1908 and jones in 1918 the acreage planted to hybrid cultivars and the number of crop species for which hybrids were generated have steadily increased in certain crops such as maize sugar beet sunflower and many vegetable crops only hybrid cultivars are nowadays developed whereas in many others like rice cotton sorghum and pearl millet the area planted to hybrid cultivars is rapidly increasing thus the commercial exploitation of heterosis was not only a benefit for farmers and consumers but also provided the foundation for the seed industry state of the art of science starting with the classical work by shull in 1908 the phenomenon of heterosis has been intensively studied at the last international conference on
