'''Upacara pembukaan [[Olimpiade Musim Dingin 2014]]''' berlangsung di [[Stadion Olimpiade Fisht]] di [[Sochi]], [[Rusia]], pada tanggal 7 Februari 2014, yang dimulai pada pukul 20:14 [[Waktu Moskow]] ([[UTC+04:00|UTC+4]]) dan selesai pukul 23:02 [[Waktu Moskow]] ([[UTC+4:00|UTC+4]]).<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.cnn.com/2013/10/30/sport/sochi-2014-venues-winter-olympics/|title=Sochi 2014: Winter Olympic venues|first=Inez|last=Torre|date=31 October 2013|accessdate=12 December 2013|publisher=CNN}}</ref> Ini adalah [[Olimpiade Musim Dingin]] dan [[Olimpiade]] pertama yang dibuka oleh [[Presiden Komite Olimpiade Internasional|Presiden IOC]] [[Thomas Bach]].<ref>{{cite web|url = http://keepingscore.blogs.time.com/2014/02/06/sochi-olympics-opening-ceremony/|title=Everything You Need to Know About The Sochi Olympics Opening Ceremony|date=6 February 2014|work=Time|accessdate=7 February 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.bbc.com/sport/0/winter-olympics/26066007|title=Russian city prepares for Olympic opening ceremony|date=6 February 2014|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=7 February 2014}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.telegraph.co.uk/sport/othersports/winter-olympics/10623697/Sochi-opening-ceremony-live.html|title=Sochi opening ceremony: live|date=7 February 2014|work=Daily Telegraph|accessdate=7 February 2014}}</ref>

Upacara ini dibuka secara resmi oleh Presiden [[Vladimir Putin]]. Sekitar 40.000 penonton hadir di stadion, dan diperkirakan 2.000 penampil ikut serta dalam pertunjukan artistik.<ref>{{cite web|last=Dassanayake |first=Dion |url=http://www.express.co.uk/news/world/458578/Winter-Olympics-Opening-ceremony-for-Sochi-2014-kicks-off-with-a-bang |title=Winter Olympics: Opening ceremony for Sochi 2014 kicks off with a bang &#124; World &#124; News &#124; Daily Express |publisher=Express.co.uk |date= |accessdate=2014-02-07}}</ref>

==Pelaksanaan==
Duo pop Rusia, [[t.A.T.u.]], tampil dalam upacara pembukaan.<ref>{{cite news|publisher= The Independent (London)|url= http://www.independent.co.uk/arts-entertainment/music/news/russian-lesbian-duo-tatu-to-perform-at-sochi-olympics-9114261.html|title= Russian 'lesbian' duo t.A.T.u to perform at Sochi Olympics?|author= Jess Denham|date= 7 February 2014 }} </ref> [[Sumpah Olimpiade]] atas nama semua atlet dibacakan oleh [[Ruslan Zakharov]] (lari jarak pendek), sedangkan [[Vyacheslav Vedenin]] dan [[Anastasia Popkova]] membacakan sumpah wasit dan pelatih. Enam pembawa obor terakhir secara berurutan adalah: [[Maria Sharapova]], [[Yelena Isinbayeva]], [[Alexander Karelin]], [[Alina Kabaeva]], [[Irina Rodnina]], dan [[Vladislav Tretiak]].<ref>http://sports.blogs.nytimes.com/2014/02/07/live-coverage-2014-winter-olympics-opening-ceremony/#updated</ref>
