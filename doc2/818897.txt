Ed Guiney
'''Ed Guiney''' (lahir 18 Februari 1966) adalah produser film Irlandia dan pendiri [[Element Pictures]] yang dikenal sebagai produser film ''[[The Lobster]]'' (2015) dan ''[[Room (film 2015)|Room]]'' (2015).<ref>{{cite web|last1=Barraclough|first1=Leo|title=Irish Producer Ed Guiney to Receive European Co-Production Award|url=http://variety.com/2014/film/awards/irish-producer-ed-guiney-to-receive-european-co-production-award-1201358190/|publisher=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|accessdate=14 January 2016}}</ref> Ia mendapat nominasi [[Academy Award for Best Picture]] untuk film ''Room''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Element Have Much to Celebrate - With Four Irish Oscar Nominations|url=http://www.iftn.ie/news/?act1=record&only=1&aid=73&rid=4288910&tpl=archnews&force=1|publisher=The Irish Film Television Network|accessdate=14 January 2016}}</ref>
