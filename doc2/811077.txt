Run for Money
'''''run for money''''' ({{lang-tr|Ka para ka}}) adalah sebuah [[Sinema Turki|film Turki]] 1999 yang di[[sutradara]]i oleh [[Reha Erdem]]. Film tersebut menjadi perwakilan Turki pada [[Academy Awards ke-73]] untuk [[Academy Award untuk Film Berbahasa Asing Terbaik]], namun tidak masuk nominasi.<ref name="46Submisisons">{{cite web|url=http://www.oscars.org/press/pressreleases/2000/00.11.20.a.html|title=Record 46 Countries in Race for Oscar|publisher=[[Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences]]|date=2000-11-20|accessdate=2008-07-19 |archiveurl = http://web.archive.org/web/20080405192710/http://www.oscars.org/press/pressreleases/2000/00.11.20.a.html <!-- Bot retrieved archive --> |archivedate = 2008-04-05}}</ref><ref name="indieWireNominees">{{cite web|url=http://www.indiewire.com/biz/biz_010213_OscarNoms.html|title=AMPAS Announces the Nominees for the 73rd Academy Awards|publisher=[[indieWire]]|accessdate=2008-07-19}} {{Dead link|date=October 2010|bot=H3llBot}}</ref>
