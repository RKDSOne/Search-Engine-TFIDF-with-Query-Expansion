'''Cody Robert Simpson''' ({{lahirmati|[[Gold Coast, Queensland]], [[Australia]]|11|1|1997}}) adalah [[penyanyi]] dan penulis lagu asal [[Gold Coast, Queensland]], [[Australia]], yang saat ini menandatangani kontrak dengan label rekaman [[Amerika Serikat]], [[Atlantic Records]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.atlanticrecords.com/codysimpson |title=Atlantic Records - Cody Simpson |publisher=atlanticrecords.com |accessdate=15 August 2010}}</ref>

== Kehidupan awal ==
Simpson lahir dari pasangan Brad dan Angie Simpson. Dia memiliki dua saudara kandung yang lebih muda, Tom dan Alli.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.goldcoast.com.au/article/2010/06/19/229321_news.html|title=The Cody Simpson Effect|last=Grant|first=Dwayne|date=19 June 2010|work=goldcoast.com.au|accessdate=9 August 2010}}</ref> Ia juga seorang perenang berbakat dan memenangkan dua medali emas di Kejuaraan Renang Queensland.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.goldcoast.com.au/article/2009/12/14/169285_gold-coast-sport.html|title=Rickard motivated by loss|last=Dew|first=Jake|date=14 December 2009|work=goldcoast.com.au|accessdate=17 May 2010}}</ref> Simpson dilatih di Klub Renang [[Miami, Queensland|Miami]] di bawah pelatih Ken Nixon.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.clubsonline.com.au/news/index.cfm?fuseaction=display_main&NewsID=11584&orgid=4033&cfid=42832036&cftoken=86561938&dts=29120105725|title=2009 Qld Championships a Huge Success!|date=22 January 2010|work=clubsonline.com.au|accessdate=18 May 2010}}</ref> Simpson ibu Angie bekerja sebagai sukarelawan di klub ini.<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.goldcoast.com.au/article/2009/05/12/77885_gold-coast-news.html|title=$500 a year to watch kids swim|last=Chambers|first=Geoff|coauthors=Dew, Jake|date=12 May 2009|work=goldcoast.com.au|accessdate=18 May 2010}}</ref>

Simpson mulai merekam lagu di kamar tidurnya selama musim panas tahun 2009 di [http://www.youtube.com/codysimpsonmusic YouTube], menampilkan "[[I'm Yours (lagu Jason Mraz)|I'm Yours]]" oleh [[Jason Mraz]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2vnp2e2O8P0 |title=Cody Simpson singing "I'm Yours" by Jason Mraz |publisher=Youtube |accessdate=8 August 2010}}</ref> "[[Cry Me a River]]"<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CkO1RoX6qWA |title=Cry Me a River - Cody Simpson Cover |publisher=Youtube |accessdate=8 August 2010}}</ref> dan "[[Seorita]]"<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h8x0quR56aU |title=Justin Timberlake - Seorita cover - Cody Simpson|publisher=Youtube|accessdate=19 August 2010}}</ref> oleh [[Justin Timberlake]], "[[I Want You Back]]" oleh [[Jackson 5]]<ref>
He also made covers of "Wanted Dead or Alive" by Bon Jovi, and "The Man Who Can't be Moved" by The Script{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xkwaAdt0Rh8 |title=I Want You Back by Michael Jackson - Cody Simpson Cover|publisher=Youtube|accessdate=19 August 2010}}</ref> dan lagunya sendiri, "One"<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=J-ZuRlvggMo |title='One' - Cody Simpson |publisher=Youtube |accessdate=8 August 2010}}</ref> dan "Perfect".<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UMZb1iP9WhY |title=Perfect - Original by Cody Simpson |publisher=Youtube |accessdate=8 August 2010}}</ref> Dia kemudian ditemukan di YouTube oleh Shawn Campbell,<ref>{{Cite news|url=http://www.couriermail.com.au/entertainment/confidential/cody-simpson-is-queenslands-answer-to-justin-bieber/story-e6freq7o-1225863802065|title=Cody Simpson is Queensland's Answer to Justin Bieber|date=7 May 2010|work=couriermail.com.au|accessdate=17 May 2010}}</ref> seorang produser rekaman yang telah beberapa kali masuk nominasi Grammy yang telah menjadi produser untuk [[Jay-Z]] dan artis lainnya.

== Karier musik ==
Peningkatan popularitas Simpson mulai mendapat pengakuan setelah tampil pada acara ''[[The 7.30 Report]]'' pada bulan Desember 2009.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.abc.net.au/7.30/content/2009/s2775128.htm|title=YouTube launches Gold Coast boy|last=Baillie|first=Rebecca|date=17 December 2009|work=abc.net.au|accessdate=16 May 2010}}</ref>
