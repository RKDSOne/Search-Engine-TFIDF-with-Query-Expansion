'''Phyllis Christine Cast'''<ref>Vit Wagner, [http://www.thestar.com/Entertainment/article/609729 "Fame's at stake for Cast of two: Mother and daughter team's vampire formula"], ''[[Toronto Star]]'', March 29, 2009.</ref> (lahir 1960) adalah penulis [[roman]]/[[fantasi]] [[Amerika Serikat]]. Dia terkenal atas karyanya, seri ''[[House of Night]]'', yang dia tulis bersama putrinya [[Kristin Cast]]. Karyanya yang lain adalah  seri ''Goddess Summoning'' dan ''Partholon''.<ref name="CTV">Constance Drogranes,[http://www.ctv.ca/servlet/ArticleNews/story/CTVNews/20090326/vampires_hunted_090326/20090326 "Vampire love spikes in new book 'Hunted'"], [[CTV News]], March 26, 2009.</ref>
