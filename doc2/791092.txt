'''Sofa Margarita Vergara Vergara'''<!-- Bukan kesalahan. Nama ayah dan ibunya sama-sama Vergara, jangan dihapus--> ({{IPA-es|sofi.a eraa}}; {{lahirmati|[[Barranquilla]], [[Atlntico Department|Atlntico]], [[Colombia]]|10|7|1972}})<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/entre-pitufos-mexicanos-sof-a-vergara-celebr-sus-39-a-os-29008 |title=Entre pitufos mexicanos, Sofa Vergara celebr sus 39 aos / Among Mexican Smurfs, Sofia Vergara celebrated her 39 years|date=July 11, 2011 |work=[[El Heraldo (Colombia)|El Heraldo]] |language=Spanish |accessdate=March 29, 2015 |quote=Sofa Margarita Vergara Vergara naci el 10 de julio de 1972 en Barranquilla. / Sofa Margarita Vergara Vergara was born on July 10, 1972 in Barranquilla. |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131004215025/http://www.elheraldo.co/tendencias/entre-pitufos-mexicanos-sof-a-vergara-celebr-sus-39-a-os-29008 |archivedate=October 4, 2013 }}</ref> adalah [[pemeran]], [[komedian]], [[produser]], pembawa acara [[televisi]], dan seorang wanita pengusaha berkebangsaan [[Colombia]]-[[Amerika Serikat]]. Namanya dikenal secara luas melalui penampilannya di program [[televisi]] berbahasa [[Spanyol]] di kanal [[Univisin]] akhir tahun [[1990]]-an. Kali pertama dia menerima peran [[film]] berbahasa [[Inggris]] adalah ''[[Chasing Papi]]'' (2003). Selanjutnya, dia tampil di film-film lain seperti ''[[Four Brothers (film)|Four Brothers]]'' (2005) dan dua film garapan [[Tyler Perry]], ''[[Meet the Browns (film)|Meet the Browns]]'' (2008) dan ''[[Madea Goes to Jail]]'' (2009), yang kemudian hari masuk nominasi dalam [[ALMA Award]]. Kesuksesan Vergara di ranah industri hiburan televisi dapat disaksikan melalui peran-perannya dalam ''[[The Smurfs (film)|The Smurfs]]'' (2011), ''[[New Year's Eve (film)|New Year's Eve]]'' (2011), ''[[Happy Feet Two]]'' (2011), ''[[The Three Stooges (2012 film)|The Three Stooges]]'' (2012), ''[[Escape from Planet Earth]]'' (2013), ''[[Machete Kills]]'' (2013), ''[[Chef (film)|Chef]]'' (2014), dan ''[[Hot Pursuit (film)|Hot Pursuit]]'' (2015). Tahun [[2012]] dan [[2013]], dia tercatat sebagai pemeran paling populer di [[televisi]] [[Amerika Serikat]].<ref name="forbes.com">[http://www.forbes.com/sites/meghancasserly/2013/09/04/sofia-vergara-is-the-top-earning-actress-on-television-by-a-longshot/ Sofia Vergara Is The Top-Earning Actress On Television... By A Longshot]. Forbes (April 9, 2013). Retrieved on 2013-09-05</ref> Vergara membintangi drama seri di kanal [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]], ''[[Modern Family]]'' sebagai [[Gloria Delgado-Pritchett]], di mana dia mendapatkan empat nominasi [[Golden Globe Award]]s, empat [[Primetime Emmy Award]], dan tujuh [[Screen Actors Guild Award]]s.<ref>{{cite web|title=
Sofa Vergara Awards|url=http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0005527/awards?ref_=nm_awd}}</ref> In 2014, she was ranked as the 32nd most powerful woman in the world by ''Forbes''.<ref name=Forbes14>{{cite web|title=The World's 100 Most Powerful Women|url=http://www.forbes.com/power-women/list/#tab:overall|website=Forbes|publisher=Forbes|accessdate=June 24, 2014}}</ref>
