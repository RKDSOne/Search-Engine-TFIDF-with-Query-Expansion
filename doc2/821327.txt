'''Harold Becker''' (kelahiran 25 September 1928) adalah seorang sutradara dan produser Amerika dari New York. Beberapa film yang ia sutradarai meliputi ''[[The Onion Field (film)|The Onion Field]]'', ''[[Taps (film)|Taps]]'', ''[[The Boost]]'', ''[[Sea of Love (film)|Sea of Love]]'',<ref>{{cite news|last=Brown|first=Joe|title=Sea of Love|work=[[Washington Post]]|url=http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-srv/style/longterm/movies/videos/seaofloverbrown_a0adaf.htm|date=15 September 1989|accessdate=26 Desember 2010}}</ref> ''[[Malice (film)|Malice]]'', ''[[City Hall (film)|City Hall]]''<ref>{{cite web|work=[[The New York Times]]|url=http://www.nytimes.com/movie/review?res=9403EFD91239F935A25751C0A960958260|title=City Hall (1996) FILM REVIEW; Dangerous Dealings In the Heart of New York|authorlink=Janet Maslin|author=Maslin, Janet|date=16 Februari 1996}}</ref> dan ''[[Mercury Rising]]''.
