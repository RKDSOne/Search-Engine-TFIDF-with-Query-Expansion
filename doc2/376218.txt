==Usage==

<code><nowiki>{{MRT locator map| station name | text after station name (optional) | px=300 (optional) }}</nowiki></code>

For stations with different filenames for the map (e.g. Botanic Gardens filename is Adam), add the corresponding line in the template. E.g:

<code><nowiki> | Botanic Gardens=Adam</nowiki></code>

Examples:

<code><nowiki>{{MRT locator map|Botanic Gardens}}</nowiki></code>

gives

{{MRT locator map|Botanic Gardens}}

and

<code><nowiki>{{MRT locator map|Sengkang|Town Centre Station in the North East|px=250}}</nowiki></code>

gives

{{MRT locator map|Sengkang|Town Centre Station in the North East|px=250}}
