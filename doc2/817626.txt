'''Alicia Amanda Vikander''' ({{IPA-sv|alisa amanda vkandr}}; lahir 3 Oktober 1988) adalah aktris dan penari Swedia. Vikander mengawali kariernya di pertunjukan teater dan sejumlah film pendek dan seri televisi Swedia, termasuk drama TV ternama ''[[Andra Avenyn]]'' (200810). Untuk film cerita, ia terlibat dalam film ''[[Pure (film 2009)|Pure]]'' (2010) dan memenangi [[Guldbagge Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role|Guldbagge Award for Best Actress]]. Vikander mendapat perhatian luas setelah muncul dalam adaptasi ''[[Anna Karenina (film 2012)|Anna Karenina]]'' tahun 2012, film Denmark ''[[A Royal Affair]]'' (2012), dan biopik [[Julian Assange]] ''[[The Fifth Estate (film)|The Fifth Estate]]'' (2013).

Pada tahun 2015, ia memerankan [[Vera Brittain]] dalam ''[[Testament of Youth (film)|Testament of Youth]]'' dan robot [[kecerdasan buatan]] bernama Ava di ''[[Ex Machina (film)|Ex Machina]]''. Atas perannya sebagai Ava, ia mendapatkan nominasi [[Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress  Motion Picture|Golden Globe]] dan [[BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Supporting Role|BAFTA]] kategori Best Supporting Actress. Atas perannya sebagai seniman [[Gerda Wegener]] dalam ''[[The Danish Girl (film)|The Danish Girl]]'', ia mendapatkan nominasi Best Actress di [[Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Motion Picture  Drama|Golden Globes]] dan [[BAFTA Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role|BAFTA]], serta Best Supporting Actress di [[Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress|Academy Awards]], [[Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Supporting Actress|Critics' Choice Awards]], dan [[Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Supporting Role|Screen Actors Guild Awards]].
