Presiden Warsawa
'''Presiden Warsawa''' adalah pimpinan [[Warsawa|ibukota Polandia]]<!-- dan counterpart of a [[mayor]] in smaller Polish and foreign cities. 

Following the ''Warsaw Act'' (''Ustawa warszawska'') of [[October 27]], [[2002]], the President of Warsaw carries over most of the executive duties in the city. His prerogative is, among others, governing the city-owned property that still constitutes a major part of the city.-->
