Pantone
[[Image:Pantone logo.svg|right|200px]]
'''Pantone Inc.''' adalah sebuah perusahaan yang bermarkas besar di [[Carlstadt, New Jersey]].<ref>Horyn, Cathy. [http://www.nytimes.com/2007/12/20/fashion/20COLOR.html "Pantone's Color of the Year Is... "], ''[[The New York Times]]'', December 20, 2007. Accessed September 8, 2008. "Nonetheless, Pantones choice of blue iris, or No. 18-3943, got some news media attention last week, which seemed to be partly the objective of the company, which is based in Carlstadt, N.J."</ref>

Pantone mulai menjadi perusahaan percetakan komersial pada 1950an.
