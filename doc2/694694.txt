Pemerintahan Yatsenyuk
'''Pemerintahan Yatsenyuk''' adalah sebuah pemeritahan sementara yang dikepalai oleh [[Arseniy Yatsenyuk]]. Pemerintah ini didirikan di [[Ukraina]] sebagai dampak dari [[Revolusi Ukraina 2014|Revolusi Ukraina]]. [[Rusia]], mengecam peristiwa tersebut dan tidak mengakui Pemerintahan Yatsenyuk sebagai pemerintahan yang sah.

Beberapa pihak, di sisi lain, mengakui pemerintahan ini, termasuk [[Kanselir]] [[Jerman]] [[Angela Merkel]], [[Perdana Menteri]] [[Latvia]] [[Laimdota Straujuma]], dan Perdana Menteri [[Lithuania]] [[Algirdas Butkeviius]] yang pada [[27 Februari]] [[2014]] memberi selamat pada Yatsenyuk pada pemilihannya sebagai Perdana Menteri.<ref>[http://www.bundesregierung.de/Content/EN/Artikel/2014/03/2014-02-28-lage-ukraine_en.html?nn=393830 Stabilising Ukraine's economy], Official website of the [[Cabinet of Germany]] (28 February 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.leta.lv/eng/home/important/96F935E6-B3C2-4B3D-ADB2-E9B0E1750842/ PM Straujuma offers her congratulations to new government in Ukraine], [[LETA]] (27 February 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.lithuaniatribune.com/64288/pm-butkevicius-congratulates-ukraines-new-prime-minister-201464288/ PM Butkeviius congratulates Ukraines new Prime Minister], [[Lithuania Tribune]] (27 February 2014)</ref> Pengakuan juga dilontarkan oleh wakil presdien [[Amerika Serikat]] [[Joe Biden]] yang mengatakan bahwa Amerika Serikat akan mendukung penuh pemerintahan ini.<ref>[http://www.themoscowtimes.com/news/article/new-ukraine-government-has-white-houses-support-us-vice-president-says/495403.html New Ukraine Government Has White House's Support, U.S. Vice President Says], [[The Moscow Times]] (28 February 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.politico.com/story/2014/02/joe-biden-ukraine-prime-minister-arseniy-yatsenyuk-104069.html Joe Biden calls new Ukraine leader, pledges support], [[Politico]] (27 February 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.voanews.com/content/biden-us-supports-ukraines-new-government/1861085.html Biden: U.S. Supports Ukraine's New Government], [[Voice of America]] (27 February 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.reuters.com/article/2014/02/28/us-ukraine-crisis-whitehouse-idUSBREA1R04W20140228 Vice President Biden calls Ukraine PM Yatseniuk, pledges U.S. support], [[Reuters]] (27 February 2014)</ref>

Beberapa hari kemudian, [[Sekretaris negara]] Amerika Serikat [[John Kerry]] mengunjungi [[Kyiv]] pada [[4 Maret]] [[2014]] dan bertemu dengan Yatsenyuk, diikuti oleh beberapa anggota [[Uni Eropa]] pada [[6 Maret]] [[2014]].<ref>[http://time.com/17258/ukrainian-prime-minister-to-visit-washington-d-c/ Ukrainian Prime Minister to Visit Washington D.C.], [[Time (magazine)]] (March 9, 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.euractiv.com/europes-east/eu-summit-lays-red-carpet-yatsen-news-533945 EU summit rolls out red carpet for Ukraine's Yatsenyuk], [[EurActiv]] (March 6, 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-2572922/John-Kerry-arrives-support-fledgling-Ukraine-government-Obama-announces-1billion-aid-package.html John Kerry arrives to show support for fledgling Ukraine government as Obama announces $1billion aid package], [[Daily Mail]] (5 March 2014)</ref><ref>[http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/mar/06/us-visa-restrictions-officials-ukraine-russia US imposes visa restrictions on Russian officials as Obama signs sanctions order], [[theguardian.com]] (6 March 2014)</ref>

== Latar belakang ==
Berawal dari [[Euromaidan|protes Euromaidan]] yang menyatakan anti pemerintah pada tahun 2013 hingga puncaknya pada [[21 Februari]] [[2014]], yakni pemakzulan Presiden [[Viktor Yanukovych]] pada [[Revolusi Ukraina 2014]].<ref name="Yanuousted">[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-26312008 Ukraine: Speaker Oleksandr Turchynov named interim president], [[BBC News]] (23 February 2014)<br>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-middle-east-26248275 Ukraine protests timeline], [[BBC News]] (23 February 2014)</ref> Pemerintahan ini lalu pertama kali muncul di basis Euromaidan di [[Kiev]] yakni [[Maidan Nezalezhnosti]] pada [[26 Februari]] [[2014]].<ref name=SCBBC26214>[http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-26359150 Ukraine crisis: Yatsenyuk is PM-designate, Kiev Maidan told], [[BBC News]] (26 February 2014)</ref> Pemeritahan ini memperoleh suara dari [[Verkhovna Rada]] pada esok harinya.<ref name=IU26214GNU/><ref>[http://www.segodnya.ua/politics/pnews/yacenyuk-nazval-novyy-sostav-kabmina-499253.html    ]</ref> Dan didalamnya tidak tercantum nama salah seorang tokoh Partai [[UDAR]], [[Vitali Klitschko]] yang juga tokoh [[Euromaidan]].<ref>[http://online.wsj.com/news/articles/SB10001424052702304071004579407372408125640?mg=reno64-wsj&url=http%3A%2F%2Fonline.wsj.com%2Farticle%2FSB10001424052702304071004579407372408125640.html Protest Leaders Pick Activists for 'Government of Unity'], [[The Wall Street Journal]] (26 February 2014)</ref><ref name=BBC1RSU>[http://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-25910834 Profile: Ukraine's key protest figures], [[BBC News]] (27 January 2014)</ref> Partai UDAR menolak tawaran untuk bergabung dalam pemerintahan baru.<ref name=Gard4314>[http://www.theguardian.com/world/2014/mar/04/who-governing-ukraine-olexander-turchynov Who exactly is governing Ukraine?], [[theguardian.com]] (4 March 2014)</ref>

Pada hari pertama berdirinya pemerintahan ini, sekitar 250 anggota parlemen menyatakan bergabung pada koalisi ini, termasuk [[Batkivshchyna]], sebagian UDAR, faksi [[Svoboda (partai politik)|Svoboda]], [[Economic Development (faksi parlemen)|Economic Development]] dan grup [[Ukraina Eropa Berdaulat]] dan lainnya.<ref>[http://en.interfax.com.ua/news/general/193195.html 250 MPs sign up to join coalition - Turchynov], [[Interfax-Ukraine]] (27 February 2014)</ref>
