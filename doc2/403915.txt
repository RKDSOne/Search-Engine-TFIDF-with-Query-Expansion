'''Vilayanur Subramanian "Rama" Ramachandran''' (lahir 1951) adalah ilmuwan [[neurosains]] yang dikenal akan karyanya dalam bidang [[neurologi perilaku]] dan [[psikofisika]]. Ia adalah kepala Center for Brain and Cognition,<ref>[http://cbc.ucsd.edu/ Center for Brain and Cognition website]</ref><ref>[http://cbc.ucsd.edu/ramabio.html Ramachandran Bio on CBC website]</ref><ref>[http://psychology.ucsd.edu/research-areas/index.html Psychology Department Webage with link to CBC]</ref> dan profesor di [[University of California, San Diego]].<ref>[http://psychology.ucsd.edu/people/faculty/faculty-gallery.html UCSD Psychology Faculty Directory]</ref><ref>[http://neurograd.ucsd.edu/faculty/detail.php?id=142 Ramachandran Neurosciences Graduate Program Webpage]</ref>

Ramachandran terkenal akan percobaannya dalam neurologi perilaku. Ia telah dijuluki sebagai "[[Marco Polo]] [[neurosains]] oleh [[Richard Dawkins]]<ref name = "Colapinto">{{cite news|title= Brain Games; The Marco Polo of Neuroscience|author= Colapinto, J|url= http://www.newyorker.com/reporting/2009/05/11/090511fa_fact_colapinto|newspaper= The New Yorker|date= May 11, 2009|accessdate=March 11, 2011}} [http://thatmarcusfamily.org/philosophy/Course_Websites/Intuitions/Readings/Colapinto-interview-Rama-NYer.pdf Full text via Lexis-Nexus here]</ref> dan "[[Paul Broca]] modern" oleh [[Eric Kandel]].<ref name = "Colapinto" /> Pada tahun 1997, majalah ''[[Newsweek]]'' menobatkannya sebagai salah satu dari "seratus orang yang paling penting untuk ditonton" pada abad ke-21,<ref>{{cite web|url = http://www.newsweek.com/1997/04/21/the-century-club.html | title = The Century Club | publisher = Newsweek |date = April 21, 1997 | accessdate = February 16, 2011}}</ref> sementara pada tahun 2011 ''[[Time (majalah)|Time]]'' memasukkannya ke dalam daftar seratus orang yang paling berpengaruh di dunia.<ref name = "2011Time100">{{cite web| url = http://www.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,2066367_2066369_2066125,00.html | title = V.S. Ramachandran - Time 100 | date = April 21, 2011 | accessdate = April 21, 2011}} The citation by Tom Insel, Director of NIH, reads: "Once described as the Marco Polo of neuroscience, V.S. Ramachandran has mapped some of the most mysterious regions of the mind. He has studied visual perception and a range of conditions, from synesthesia to autism. V.S. Ramachandran is changing how our brains think about our minds."</ref><ref>In poll results used for the 2011 list Ramachandran ranked 97 out of 100.[http://www.time.com/time/specials/packages/article/0,28804,2058044_2061021_2061023,00.html]</ref>
