Dirk Wilutzky
'''Dirk Wilutzky''' (lahir 1965 di [[Herleshausen]], Jerman) adalah produser dan sutradara film Jerman. Ia memenangkan [[Academy Awards|Academy Award]] dalam kategori [[Academy Award for Best Documentary Feature|Best Documentary Feature]] untuk film dokumenter ''[[Citizenfour]]'' (2014) pada penganugerahan [[Academy Awards ke-87]] tahun 2015.<ref>{{cite news|title=Secrets become history: Citizenfour Oscar is latest honour for Snowden journalists|url=http://www.themalaymailonline.com/showbiz/article/secrets-become-history-citizenfour-oscar-is-latest-honour-for-snowden-journ|accessdate=February 23, 2015|publisher=themalaymailonline.com|date=February 23, 2015}}</ref> Ia menikah dengan penyunting film [[Mathilde Bonnefoy]];<ref name="Packer 2014">{{cite news|last1=Packer|first1=George|authorlink1=George Packer|title=Laura Poitrass closeup view of Edward Snowden.|url=http://www.newyorker.com/magazine/2014/10/20/holder-secrets|accessdate=December 12, 2014|work=[[The New Yorker]]|date=October 20, 2014}}</ref> ia dan Bonnefoy merupakan produser ''Citizenfour''.
